Voice & Tone

How we communicate shapes how people feel about the GRT.

We are

  • Confident — We believe in this vision
  • Clear — We explain simply
  • Local — We know Rochester
  • Practical — We focus on what works

We avoid

  • "Revolutionary" / "Game-changing"
  • Tech jargon and buzzwords
  • Over-promising timelines
  • Talking down to the reader

Example

Instead of:

"Our revolutionary transit solution leverages cutting-edge technology to transform the urban mobility landscape."

Write:

"Water taxis on the Genesee can get you from RIT to downtown in 20 minutes—faster than driving at rush hour."
